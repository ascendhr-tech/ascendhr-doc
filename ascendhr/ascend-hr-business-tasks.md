## üìã 3. Functional Task List

### A. Strategy & Corporate Development
*Focus: Long-term direction and competitive positioning.*
- [ ] **Market Positioning Report:** Deep analysis of the "Talent Intelligence" market vs. traditional HRIS (Standard B2B approach).
- [ ] **Competitive Matrix:** Benchmarking vs. Workday, BambooHR, and ChartHop.
- [ ] **Business Model Canvas:** Finalize revenue streams (Per seat vs. Feature-based tiers).
- [ ] **Investor Pitch Deck:** Prepare a standard 12-slide deck for future funding/stakeholder alignment.

### B. Product Management (Business Layer)
*Focus: Alignment between market needs and feature development.*
- [ ] **Product Roadmap v1.0:** Define the transition from "Squad Planner" to "Talent Intelligence Suite".
- [ ] **Feature ROI Analysis:** Prioritize the development of "Formation View" vs. "Scouting" based on user demand.
- [ ] **Customer Feedback System:** Establish a dedicated Discord or Slack community for real-time user feedback (Inspired by Lovable/Manus).
- [ ] **User Persona Definition:** Detailed profiles for HR Managers, CEOs, and Department Leads.

### C. Marketing & Go-To-Market (GTM)
*Focus: Brand awareness, demand generation, and community building.*
- [ ] **Build in Public Strategy:** Develop a content calendar for sharing progress on LinkedIn and X/Twitter.
- [ ] **Waitlist Optimization:** Implement a referral mechanism on the landing page to drive viral growth.
- [ ] **Content Marketing Pillars:**
    - "The Future of Team Visualization" (Thought Leadership).
    - "Gap Analysis Case Studies" (Educational).
- [ ] **Brand Identity System:** Refine professional visual assets (Logo, Typography, Brand Voice Guide).

### D. Growth & Analytics
*Focus: Data-driven experimentation and scale.*
- [ ] **North Star Metric Definition:** e.g., "Active Team Visualizations per Week".
- [ ] **AARRR Funnel Implementation:** Set up PostHog/Mixpanel to track every stage from landing page to retention.
- [ ] **Referral Loop Design:** Create "In-App Invites" for managers to invite their leadership teams.
- [ ] **Pricing Sensitivity Research:** Conduct interviews to determine the optimal price point for tech agencies.

### E. Operations, Legal & Finance
*Focus: Regulatory compliance and organizational stability.*
- [ ] **Data Privacy & Compliance (GDPR/SOC2):** Draft the roadmap for data security, essential for B2B HR tools.
- [ ] **Operational Budgeting:** Track burn rate and resource allocation for the next 6 months.
- [ ] **Terms of Service & Privacy Policy:** Professional legal documentation for pilot and beta launch.
- [ ] **Hiring Roadmap:** Define the next key business hires (e.g., Growth Lead, Customer Success).
